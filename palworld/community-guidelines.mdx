title: "Community Guidelines"

These guidelines apply on our Palworld server and exist to help everybody get along and have fun together!

Make sure you are familiar with our global [network rules](/rules) as well!

# Social Conduct

Following these points will help you have positive interactions with other players.

- Be nice. Being a nuisance can get you in trouble and won't make you very popular!
- Give others a chance. Repeatedly camping skill fruit trees and alpha spawn points limits other players' access to these resources and you should give them a chance to have a go.
- Don't be elitist. Everybody has a different amount of time to play and you shouldn't treat members differently because they are a higher or lower level than you.
- Offer to help before helping. Some players might appreciate a hand, but others might want to play on their own.

# Builds and Bases

To encourage community interaction, building decay is disabled. You can safely build outside your base's claim area and create communal hubs and infrastructure.

- Don't place random base parts for the sake of it.
- Don't build too close to other people's creations without asking permission.
- Don't wall off or build your base around shared content such as shops, skill fruit trees or alpha spawn points.
- Keep your valuables at home. An unprotected chest can be looted easily!
- Don't intentionally try to lag the server. Lag causing builds can be removed without notice.

## Shared Content

Players are encouraged to build communal infrastructure to help out their fellow players.

When making shared content:

- Clearly mark when something you've built is for the community.
- Clearly describe what the players can do with it. Can they take resources for free, or do they need to put something back in return?

When accessing shared content:

- Don't hog everything! Shared content is made for everybody.
- Make sure you check what you can do with shared content before you assume.

## Abandoned Builds

If you find an unprotected build and you think it's been abandoned by its original owner, ask a staff member if you can claim it.

Builds inside a base claim will not be considered abandoned.

# Final Note

Our community is built on a foundation of trust and mutual respect. By following these guidelines, every player is on the same page.

Repeated violations of community guidelines may result in whitelist removal.
